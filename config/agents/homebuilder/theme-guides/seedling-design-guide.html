04 — Voice

Patient + precise

No hand-holding. No exclamation points. The gardener has seen things grow and die.

"Your presence hasn't been tended in 14 days. Three peers hold your last signature. Shall I propagate, or will you guide the content first?"

Never: urgent

No "ACTION REQUIRED". No red banners. Status is informational, never coercive.

✗ "Your profile is OUT OF DATE! Update NOW!"

✗ "You have 7 unread notifications — don't miss out!"

Technical, not technocratic

Show hashes, peer IDs, CRDT state. Frame through the garden. Both coexist.

"4 of 7 peers confirm the merge. The lattice is reconciling — the signal is propagating outward."

Never: corporate-friendly

No "Great!", no emojis in system output, no SaaS onboarding energy.

✗ "Welcome to Seedling! 🌱 We're so excited!"

✗ "Let's get you set up in just 3 easy steps!"
05 — Hero · 3D Scene Concept

Full-viewport 3D scene. The camera and geometry respond to the user — the product's metaphor is revealed through interaction, not copy.

State A — load
Camera ≈ 30° above horizontal.

Dark matte plane — graphene/granite, perspective grid. Title floats above surface. Saplings barely visible. Seed hinted underground.
Transition — click anywhere
Camera pivots: 30° → 0°, 900ms ease-in-out-cubic.

Ground cross-section becomes visible. Seed exposed below horizon. Stalk rises. Two flanking saplings suggest network presence.
State B — revealed
Seed (underground): hover grows a download root downward — CTA appears.

Stalk leaves: hover each to reveal nav branches.

Flanking saplings: suggest the wider lattice.
Material
  • — Ground: matte black · faint orthographic grid · no specularity
  • — Seed: dark ellipsoid · ice inner glow on hover
  • — Stalks + leaves: near-black geometry · ice outline on hover
  • — Sky (above horizon): void — pure black
  • — One cold directional light · no shadows
  • — Three.js / WebGL · CSS 3D fallback
Interaction rules
  • — Transition: 900ms · ease-in-out-cubic
  • — Hover: ice outline traces shape · CTA grows from root
  • — Hover on proximity, not click
  • — Scroll past scene → fade to page content
  • — Mobile: tap ground → transition · tap seed → download
  • — Reduced motion: skip pivot · show State B immediately
